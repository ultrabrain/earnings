Earnings dates: estimated (amber) dates are approximated to the company's historical reporting week within the fiscal quarter and are refined as they're confirmed; the countdown is computed live each time you open the page.
Market data (Last, Chg, YTD, 3M Vol, Support, Resist) is computed by update_market_data.py from a moomoo / Futu OpenD daily K-line export (moomoo_history.csv) in this folder. 3M Vol = annualised standard deviation of daily log returns over the last 63 trading days. Support / Resist = the lowest low / highest high of the last 26 weekly bars. These columns show — until the moomoo export is present, then refresh daily. Resist turns blue when the last price is at the highest point in the exported history (a new high). Support or Resist blinks when the last price is within 3% of that level.
Watchlist: add or remove tickers in the bar up top — your list is saved in this browser. New tickers appear under “Pending” until the next data refresh; click Save watchlist.txt into this folder so the daily task and moomoo export track the same names.
ETFs in your watchlist (SMH, SKYY, CIBR, IGV, CHAT) are excluded as they do not report earnings.
